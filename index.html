ABOUT

Amanda Brook is an artist and designer who likes to combine the sensibilities of tradtional and digital art. She is especially interested in channeling and studying stong emotions through imagery.

Whether it's the love of a pet or a dream that dredges up your deepest fears, those images become ingrained and imprinted within out minds. Imagery and language can become a reminder of such things. Combining an imprinted memory with a stylized emotional warping can serve to amplify these emotions that rise to our consciousness.
